    Social Activities

      1. Seva Vasti Survey – Formulated questions for survey in Slums of Nashik during June 2021 to May 2022. The primary objective of the survey was socio-economic conditions of the people living in slums in Nashik City. Mentored 5 teams of 20 MBA students leaded by a faculty each. The survey was analysed using SPSS-AMOS software and report was submitted to the CHME Society for further action.
      2. Shramanubhav Shibir – Mentored two days residential camp of MBA and MCA students of DMI in Tribal Area of Nashik. The camp is termed as Shramanubhav Shibir and aims to build primary bunds on the water streams in Nashik District. The technical and local support is given by an NGO, Janjati Kalyan Ashram, Nashik. The Shramanubhav Shibir activity is a yearly activity which started a decade ago and involves 100 students along with 10 young, energetic and enthusiastic faculties.
